SENTINEL: Sentiment Evolution and Narrative Tracking in Extended LLM Interactions
Pranav Anuraag, Ethan Xu, Alexander Arutchev, Asher Nerenberg · Algoverse AI Research

We show that emotional hallucinations in LLMs follow a distinct dynamic from factual ones, emerging in oscillatory bursts rather than accumulating linearly. SENTINEL introduces three complementary metrics (MAD, Variance, Drift Propagation Index) to quantify sentiment drift across five frontier LLMs. Token-level Integrated Gradients attribution reveals that just 12 tokens account for 73.8% of sentiment-bearing signal across 3,092 highlighted tokens, offering practical levers for mitigation in high-stakes agentic workflows.
